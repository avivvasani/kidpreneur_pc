About Kidpreneur

Empowering young innovators through CodeAGS

Our Mission

Kidpreneur is designed to spark creativity and entrepreneurial thinking in students. It’s more than a project — it’s a movement that nurtures problem solvers of tomorrow.

How It Works

Students submit ideas, get inspired by others, and collaborate in a community built on innovation. Every project helps them learn teamwork, leadership, and practical business sense.

Powered by CodeAGS

CodeAGS provides the platform, tools, and support that makes Kidpreneur possible — combining technology, design, and education for maximum impact.
